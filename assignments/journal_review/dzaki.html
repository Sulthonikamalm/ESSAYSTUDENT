Journal Article Review

Submitted by Ahmad Dzaki Zayyan Sugianto

Article Title: The Psychology of Microtransactions: Fairness in Competitive Gaming

Summary: This paper investigates the psychological impact of 'Pay-to-Win' mechanics on player retention and community toxicity. It argues that while such models increase short-term revenue, they degrade the long-term integrity of competitive esports. The review suggests alternative monetization strategies that preserve game balance.
