Article Title: The Impact of Deep Learning on Modern Healthcare

Summary: This article discusses the transformative potential of AI in medical diagnostics. It highlights how deep learning algorithms can analyze medical images with higher accuracy than human radiologists in some cases. The study specifically focuses on early detection of certain cancers and the ethical implications of relying on automated systems for critical health decisions.
