Article Title: Electronic Health Records: Challenges and Opportunities in Developing Countries

Summary: This review analyzes the current state of Electronic Medical Records (EMR) implementation in Indonesia. It identifies key infrastructure challenges such as internet connectivity in rural areas and data privacy concerns. The article proposes a phased adoption strategy to ensure sustainability and interoperability across different healthcare providers.
