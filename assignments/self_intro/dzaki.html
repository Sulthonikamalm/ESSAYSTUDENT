Hello!

I want to become a reliable web developer who is capable of creating useful applications or websites for the community. In my opinion, the world of the web is currently growing rapidly and will always be needed in various aspects of life, starting from education, business, to public services. Therefore, I consider web development as a promising field with broad opportunities in the future. Through my studies in Information Technology, I hope to deepen my understanding of programming, system development, and problem-solving skills that are essential in building high-quality web-based solutions.
