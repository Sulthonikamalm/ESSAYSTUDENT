Self-Introduction Paragraph

A creative introduction exploring personal background and academic goals.

Hello!

My name is Lidya Ammalia Suharto. I’ve loved games since I was little, and this hobby has remained an important part of my life. For me, playing games isn’t just a way to kill time, but rather a fun way to relieve stress after a busy day. It feels like being transported to another world full of colors, challenges, fun, and always something new to spark my curiosity. Through games, I’ve also learned many things, such as how to stay focused, be patient, and make quick decisions in difficult situations. I do lose sometimes, but that makes me realize that failure is normal, because it gives me the chance to try again with a smarter approach.

What makes it even more exciting is that playing games isn’t always a solitary activity. I often meet new people through online communities, whether they’re from other cities or even from other countries. By chatting and playing together, I’ve learned to be more open to differences, whether in playing styles, personalities, or cultures. I’ve also come to understand that cooperation and communication are crucial, because when playing as a team, victory can’t be achieved alone.

It’s the shared moments of success that bring the greatest satisfaction. For me, gaming has become more than just entertainment. This hobby has helped me grow into someone more patient, more creative, and more open to exploring new things in life. If you’ve never tried it, I encourage you to give gaming a chance, you might discover the same joy and growth that I’ve experienced.
