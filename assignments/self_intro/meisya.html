Self-Introduction Paragraph

A creative introduction exploring personal background and academic goals.

Hello!

Hi everyone! Let me introduce myself. My name is Meisya Ma’rifa Nur, and one of the most interesting things about me is my love for watching movies. For me, they are more than just entertainment; they are a way to explore new ideas. Among many genres, the one I like the most is thriller. Thriller movies are very exciting because they are full of tension, mystery, and surprises. I love how they make me curious and keep me thinking about what will happen next. Sometimes I even try to guess the ending, but the twists usually surprise me in a good way.

Watching thrillers is not only fun but also useful. This genre often shows interesting sides of human behavior, trust, and choices in difficult situations. It makes me think more carefully and pay attention to small details. For example, psychological thrillers make me try to understand the characters’ minds, while crime thrillers let me imagine how detectives solve complex cases.

That is why I want to persuade you to watch thriller movies too. You do not need any special skills, only curiosity and an open mind. It can be a great way to enjoy your free time, and at the same time, you may learn something new about people and life. In the future, I plan to watch more thriller films from different countries and maybe share some recommendations with friends. I believe this hobby can bring not only fun but also a fresh perspective to anyone who tries it.
